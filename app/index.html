About

The chart shows how quickly the daylight hours duration i.e. time from sunrise till sunset changes over the year.

The vertical scale is in minutes and seconds and shows the difference between today and yesterday for each day of a year.

Location data by ipinfo.io, maps by OpenStreetMap
